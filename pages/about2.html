About Rocco Cornacchia

Rocco Cornacchia, born in Italy and raised in Toronto, brings a unique blend of Italian charm and Canadian warmth to every performance. His rich baritone voice, inspired by legends like Johnny Cash, Willie Nelson, and Frank Sinatra, creates a timeless atmosphere perfect for life’s special moments (performing beloved cover songs from these and other great artists).

Rocco has been married for decades to the lovely Gail, his lifelong love since adolescence. Their enduring relationship is a true reflection of the romance and passion that inspire his music.

With years of experience captivating audiences, Rocco now offers live performances for weddings, private parties, and special events. His heartfelt singing and engaging presence ensure every celebration feels personal and unforgettable. Whether you're planning an elegant wedding reception or an intimate gathering, Rocco’s music adds a touch of classic sophistication, making your event truly memorable.

Contact Rocco today to inquire about availability and let his music bring your special occasion to life.
